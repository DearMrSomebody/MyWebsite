NETHERMORE

Young Adult Fantasy Romance

Completed Menuscript

On the outskirts of a murderous forest lived a teenage girl named Snow. She is forced to travel into the forest when she discovers her best friend has gone missing. Little did she know, that choice would destroy her life. Piece by piece, she uncovers dark secrets from her past, she was abandoned as a baby, her biological parents are killers, her friends are not what they seem, and one handsome stranger is the key to it all.

SMALL TOWN MAGIC

Young Adult Fantasy

Work-in-progress

After having the worst year of her life, Ellie was forced to sell everything and move across country to her mom's hometown in South Carolina. Between the fake southern charm and bible thumpers, Ellie felt lost until she discovered a lost journal with all sorts of weird town myths. When strange things start to happen she realized there might be more to this small town than meets the eye.

RAIN

Horror

Work-in-progress

Echo hated when it rained, every since she was a baby. Storms would uncontrollably terrify her. Her mother put her on medication that stopped the episodes, the downside was it also made her barely functioning. Barely able to move or speak, she was helpless. There was nothing she could do when her mother's boyfriend abused her, or when her best friend was beaten into a coma trying to defend her. But when the rain creatures appear, Echo learns that she is able to do more than she thought.

FOREVER, LYN

Historical Romance

Work-in-progress

Lyn is outspoken, indepentent, and strong. She has to be considering her one true love, Phantosos, only exists within her imagination. Despite the ill words from others, Lyn truely believes he is real and that one day, she will meet him. Her life changes when she befriends the only man that actually believes her tale. But when he falls for her, Lyn will have to choose between her true love and her best friend.
